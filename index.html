Road and Pothole Recognition

Pothole and cracks detection is critical in the process of maintaining and preserving roads and highways. This project focus on using Convolution Neural Networks (CNN) in road and pothole recognition. The constructed model achieved a 93% of accuracy (for testing dataset) on detecting just potholes, and 77% of accuracy (also for testing dataset) on differentiating between potholes, cracks, and good road.

Python, Neural Networks, Data Augmentation

Alzheimer's Disease Early Diagnosis

● Early and accurate diagnosis of Alzheimer’s diseaseis still an unsolved problem. This research applied machine learning on a non-evasive, early diagnostic tool called "Clock Drawing Test", which assess a patients level of cognitiveimpairment and help practitioners diagnose Alzheimer’s disease.
● Used three feature selection techniques to extractmost important features for a diagnosis: a genetic algorithm,a wrapper feature selection method, and a hierarchical archi-tecture feature selection algorithm.
● The selected features arethen used to train two different classifiers: a neural networkand a stacked generalization.
● Theresults indicate a differential diagnosis accuracy of high 70%to mid 80%are achievable.

Python, Features Selection Algorithm, Neural Networks
